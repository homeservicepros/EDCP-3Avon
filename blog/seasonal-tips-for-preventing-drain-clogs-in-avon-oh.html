Spring Drain Maintenance Tips

Spring cleaning should include your drains! After winter thaws, it's crucial to:

  • Clear outdoor drainage systems of accumulated debris
  • Check basement drains for spring rainfall readiness
  • Schedule professional sewer cleaning and maintenance to prevent backups

Summer Drain Care Essentials

Summer activities can strain your plumbing system. Keep these tips in mind:

  • Install drain strainers to catch beach sand and pool debris
  • Regularly clean outdoor grill areas to prevent grease buildup
  • Monitor basement drains during heavy summer storms

Fall Prevention Strategies

As leaves fall in Avon and Westlake, protect your drains with these steps:

  • Clear gutters and downspouts regularly
  • Remove fallen leaves from outdoor drain covers
  • Consider professional hydro jetting services before winter

Winter Drain Protection

Ohio winters can be harsh on plumbing. Prevent problems by:

  • Insulating exposed pipes
  • Running hot water regularly through rarely used drains
  • Knowing the location of your main water shutoff valve

Year-Round Prevention Tips

Follow these maintenance practices throughout the year:

  • Never pour grease down kitchen drains
  • Use drain strainers in all sinks
  • Schedule regular maintenance checks
  • Address slow drains immediately before they become complete clogs
